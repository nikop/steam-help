How can I get information on or assistance with something I ordered from the Valve store?

All Valve Store orders are currently processed by [i]For Fans By Fans[/i]. For more information or support with a Valve Store order, please contact ForFansByFans directly via one of the methods listed on this page: [url=https://www.forfansbyfans.com/contact-us]https://www.forfansbyfans.com/contact-us[/url].

Please also include the following information with your message:
[list]
[*]Your Valve Store Sales Order number [/*]
[*]The email address you used when you placed the order [/*]
[*]A brief description of your issue [/*]
[/list]
Questions about games purchased on Steam should be sent to [url=https://help.steampowered.com/en/faqs/view/6F69-0324-B2DB-6E7E]Steam Support[/url].
