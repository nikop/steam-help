Missing CD Key
--------------
Updated 2021-12-02 23:19:46
Version 5

[h1]Game purchased through Steam[/h1][h4]Valve games[/h4]Valve games purchased online through Steam do not include or require a CD key.
[h4]Third party games[/h4]CD keys are issued with many third party games.  In many cases, Steam will display your CD key when you first launch your game.  If your game launches and asks for a CD key you do not have, please try the following:
[olist]
[*]Right-click on the game in the [b]Library[/b] tab and select [b]Manage[/b] then [b]CD keys[/b]. This option is not available for all Steam games and a CD key is not required for all games. [/*]
[*][b]Verify integrity of game files[/b].  Follow the directions in the [url=https://help.steampowered.com/en/faqs/view/0C48-FCBD-DA71-93EB]Verify Integrity of Game Files[/url] article.  [/*]
[/olist]
If you are still seeing the issue or have a different issue with the key, please see the [url=https://help.steampowered.com/en/faqs/view/2FF7-8298-9409-BC1D]Steam-Issued Activation Keys[/url] page for more information.
[h1]Game purchased through a retail store[/h1]If your game did not come with a CD key where there should be one printed, you will need to contact the retail store you purchased from to discuss a refund or exchange.
