Invalid or Damaged CD Key from Merchandise
------------------------------------------
Updated 2021-07-10 19.49.55

What do I do if I receive an invalid CD Key error or have a damaged CD Key from merchandise (T-shirts, plush, pins)?

If you are receiving an invalid or duplicate error when activating a CD Key you received from merchandise, please [url=https://help.steampowered.com/]submit a ticket to Steam Support[/url].

Please also include the following information with your question, if applicable:

[list]  	[*]Your Valve Store Order number[/*]  	[*]A receipt from the third party vendor where the item was purchased[/*]  	[*]The email address you used when you placed the order[/*]  	[*][b]Please send us a photo of the CD Key that is providing the error.[/b][/*]  [/list]
