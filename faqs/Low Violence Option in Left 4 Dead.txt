Low Violence Option in Left 4 Dead

How can I lower the violence and content for Left 4 Dead?

[section style=important]  [h3]Important:[/h3]Some countries require low violence to be permanently enabled. If you live in a country where low violence settings are required, you will be unable to remove the low violence version.[/section]  [h4]Launch in Low Violence Mode[/h4][olist]      [*]Locate Left 4 Dead in your Library and right-click the game.[/*]      [*]Select "Properties..."[/*]      [*]Go to the [b]Launch Options[/b] section on the General tab in the Properties window.[/*]  [/olist]

In the Launch Options field, type "-lv" as shown in the example below:

[img]{STEAM_CLAN_IMAGE}/1976/4bc0a402f254c490f39759406ff68d57d0b26363.png[/img]

Close the Properties window, and then launch Left 4 Dead. The game should run with less bodies, limbs, and other content.
